Food Time: Rice

Ounces of Water needed.

White Rice

Combine 8 oz of rice with 16 oz of water and 1 Tbsp olive oil. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to the lowest setting. Cook for about 18 minutes.

California Rice

For slightly al dente rice: Combine 10 oz of rice with 16 oz of water or broth and 1 Tbsp olive oil. Bring to a boil and stir once to mix. Reduce heat to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook for 25 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and serve.
